ChannelMate

AI-powered YouTube comment automation for creators and businesses.

ChannelMate is an AI-powered YouTube comment automation SaaS for creators and businesses. Using secure Google OAuth and YouTube API access, users can connect their own YouTube channel to automate comment moderation, generate approved replies, manage viewer engagement, detect spam and harmful comments, and review channel analytics.

ChannelMate uses Google's official OAuth 2.0 authentication to securely connect your YouTube account. After permission is granted, ChannelMate can read comments from your YouTube videos, read video metadata such as video IDs, titles, thumbnails, publish dates, and engagement counts, read channel information and analytics needed for engagement reports, generate AI-powered replies, detect spam or abusive comments, publish approved automated replies, and display YouTube comment, video, and channel analytics.

ChannelMate never requests or stores your Google password. Your Google data is accessed only after you explicitly grant permission, is used only to provide the automation, moderation, engagement management, and analytics features you choose, and is not sold or shared with third parties. You can revoke access anytime from your Google Account.
