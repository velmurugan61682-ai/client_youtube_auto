ChannelMate

AI-powered YouTube comment automation for creators and businesses.

Connect your YouTube channel securely using Google OAuth. ChannelMate helps creators automatically reply to comments, moderate spam and abusive content, manage audience engagement, and view channel analytics.

ChannelMate requests access to your YouTube channel's comments, videos, and analytics data solely to provide automated reply, moderation, and analytics features. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Users grant permission before ChannelMate accesses YouTube data, and users can revoke access at any time from their Google Account.
