ChannelMate

AI-powered YouTube comment automation for creators and businesses.

ChannelMate is a SaaS tool that helps YouTube creators automate comment replies, moderate spam and harmful comments, manage viewer engagement, and view channel analytics after the creator securely connects their own YouTube channel.

ChannelMate uses Google OAuth and the YouTube Data API to request only the access needed for these features: YouTube comments and comment metadata, video metadata such as video IDs, titles, thumbnails, publish dates and engagement counts, channel information, and channel analytics used for engagement reports.

This data is used to fetch comments, detect spam or harmful content, generate and publish approved replies, organize comments by video and channel, and show analytics in the ChannelMate dashboard. ChannelMate does not sell or share Google or YouTube data with third parties. Users can revoke access at any time from their Google Account permissions.
