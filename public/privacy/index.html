ChannelMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

ChannelMate is a SaaS tool for YouTube creators, businesses, and agencies that helps automate comment replies, moderate comments, manage engagement, and understand channel performance.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or connect a YouTube channel through Google OAuth and the YouTube Data API, ChannelMate may collect account details, YouTube channel data, YouTube video metadata, YouTube comments and comment metadata, YouTube analytics and engagement data, encrypted OAuth tokens, moderation logs, automated reply history, sync status, and security audit events.

Why We Use It

We use this information to fetch and organize comments, generate and publish approved replies, detect spam or harmful comments, support moderation workflows, manage viewer engagement, show analytics and reports, secure accounts, troubleshoot errors, and provide customer support.

No Sale or Third-Party Sharing

ChannelMate does not sell, rent, trade, or share your Google user data, YouTube channel data, comments, video metadata, analytics data, or OAuth tokens with third parties for advertising, resale, profiling, or unrelated purposes.

Retention

We keep account records, connected channel records, OAuth tokens, cached comments, video metadata, analytics snapshots, moderation logs, and reply logs while your ChannelMate account or channel connection is active and the information is needed to provide the service. If you disconnect a channel, we delete stored OAuth tokens and stop syncing new YouTube data. Operational records are removed from active systems within a reasonable period unless limited retention is required for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, legal compliance, or backup recovery.

Revoke Access and Delete Data

You can revoke Google OAuth access at any time from Google Account permissions. To request deletion of your ChannelMate account or stored YouTube data, email support@channelbot.in from the email address associated with your account.

Contact

Questions and deletion requests: support@channelbot.in.
